Paris, France


Paris, often called the "City of Light", is one of the most popular holiday destinations in the world. Known for its rich history, art, architecture, and delicious cuisine, Paris offers a unique blend of culture and modern charm that attracts millions of visitors every year.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Eiffel Tower – The most iconic landmark of Paris.
  2. Louvre Museum – Home to the famous Mona Lisa.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral – A masterpiece of Gothic architecture.
  4. Champs-Élysées – A world-famous avenue for shopping and strolling.
  5. Seine River Cruise – A beautiful way to see Paris at night.

Must-Try Foods

  • Croissants – Flaky, buttery pastries that are perfect for breakfast.
  • Baguette – Traditional French bread with a crispy crust.
  • Crêpes – Thin pancakes filled with sweet or savory delights.
  • Ratatouille – A flavorful vegetable stew from southern France.
  • Macarons – Colorful, delicate sweets with rich fillings.
